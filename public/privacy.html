Privacy Policy for SouvieShelf

Effective date: December 19, 2025

SouvieShelf (“SouvieShelf,” “we,” “us”) helps you catalog travel souvenirs by saving photos, notes, locations, and trips. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and login)

User Content

  • Souvenir details you enter (e.g., names, notes, categories)
  • Trip details you enter (e.g., trip name, dates)
  • Photos you upload

Location Information

  • If you choose to add a location to a souvenir, we store the location you provide (such as city/country and coordinates).
  • If you use “Current Location,” we access your device’s location to help fill in location details and coordinates.

Device/Technical Information

  • We may collect limited technical information necessary to operate the app (for example, basic error logs). We do not use this information for advertising or cross-app tracking.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your souvenirs, trips, and map pins
  • Provide features you request (e.g., using current location for a souvenir)
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the app

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only in these cases:

  • Service providers: We use third-party service providers to operate the app, including:
    • Supabase (authentication and database storage)
    • Mapbox (maps and geocoding features, where applicable)
    These providers process data on our behalf to provide the service.
  • Legal reasons: If required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and user content for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion as described below.

Your Choices

Location Permissions

  • You can choose not to grant location access. You can still add locations manually.

Access and Deletion

  • You can sign out at any time.
  • If you would like to delete your account and associated data, contact us at: souvieshelf@gmail.com

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

SouvieShelf is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact: souvieshelf@gmail.com
